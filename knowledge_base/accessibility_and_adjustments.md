# Source label: accessibility_and_adjustments.md

If disability, health, accessibility or reasonable-adjustment circumstances may be relevant, describe the factual barrier and its connection to the parking event without disclosing more personal information than needed. Keep supporting evidence proportionate: for example, a Blue Badge, appointment record, accessible-format request, or a short factual explanation where appropriate.

Do not assume that a Blue Badge or medical condition automatically cancels a notice. Ask the issuer to consider the specific circumstances and any relevant adjustment, and retain a copy of the request and reply. Use the official appeal route and any accessibility contact method provided by the issuer.

Official background on service-provider adjustments: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/equality-act-guidance/disability-quick-start-guide-for-service-providers-html
