# Source label: anpr_and_timing.md

ANPR and camera systems record vehicle entry and exit times that operators use to calculate stay length. Common issues include double-dip (two separate visits treated as one long stay), clock drift, obscured plates, and mismatched payment-session times.

When challenging timing, compare the notice timestamps with payment records, location receipts, dashcam or phone timeline evidence, and any site entry/exit photos. Ask whether the operator considered separate visits or payment windows. Keep claims factual and supported.
