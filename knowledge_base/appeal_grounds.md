# Source label: appeal_grounds.md

Possible appeal grounds can include unclear signage, payment made but not recognised, app or machine failure, grace period or short stay, ANPR double-dip, incorrect vehicle details, valid permit not recognised, Blue Badge or disability-related circumstances, emergency, loading or unloading, and cautious keeper or driver process points for private notices.

Each ground should be supported by facts and evidence. ParkPal should describe grounds as possible, not guaranteed.
