# Source label: appeal_pack_quality_checklist.md

An appeal pack should be easy for a reviewer to follow. Put the notice reference, vehicle registration, issuer, route, deadline and requested outcome on the first page. Follow with a short timeline, then each appeal point paired with the exact supporting evidence.

Suggested order: original notice; appeal or challenge text; evidence index; labelled photos/screenshots; receipts, permits or official documents; correspondence and submission proof; rejection letter and escalation code where relevant. Give each attachment a clear name, date and one-line explanation of what it shows.

Before export, check that no personal details from unrelated documents are included, every claim has a matching attachment, dates are consistent, links work, and the final draft asks for a specific outcome. Retain the original files separately from the generated pack.
