# Source label: blue_badge_disability.md

Blue Badge and disability-related parking circumstances can be relevant where local rules or site terms allow badge holders certain permissions, or where a disability affected ability to comply with payment or signage requirements. Rules vary by location and site.

Evidence may include a Blue Badge photo (sensitive — share carefully), clock disc where required, medical appointment proof, and photos showing badge display. Do not overstate entitlements; check the specific site or council rules that apply.
