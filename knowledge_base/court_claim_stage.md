# Source label: court_claim_stage.md

If a parking matter escalates to a court claim, Letter Before Claim, or solicitor demand, the risk profile changes. Deadlines, forms, and defence strategy should be checked carefully. Ignoring a claim can lead to default judgment.

ParkPal should flag urgent human or qualified legal review, preserve all notices and evidence, and avoid casual admissions. Informational checklists are not a substitute for advice from a solicitor or authorised adviser.
