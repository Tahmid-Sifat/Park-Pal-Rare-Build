# Source label: deadline_safety_playbook.md

Treat every deadline as unverified until it is visible on the notice or official portal. Record the source text, date, time zone if stated, date received, and whether the date is for payment, discount, first appeal, formal representation, escalation or evidence upload.

Use calendar reminders as prompts, not proof of the deadline. Set an early evidence reminder and a separate submission reminder. Keep a screenshot or confirmation showing successful submission; a drafted appeal is not a submitted appeal.

Never assume that an appeal preserves a discount, pauses enforcement, or extends a deadline. Check the notice and route guidance. If the deadline is unclear or very close, prioritise the official route and seek direct clarification rather than waiting for more polished wording.
