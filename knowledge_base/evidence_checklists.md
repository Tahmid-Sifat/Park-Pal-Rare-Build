# Source label: evidence_checklists.md

Evidence should prove one specific point, not merely show that a visit happened. Save originals, record when and where photographs were taken, and submit copies unless the official process asks for something else.

Signage or markings: close-up photographs that can be read, wide shots showing placement/visibility, entrance signs, bay/road markings, and a photo from the driver's approach. Payment or app failure: payment confirmation, app/session history, bank record, screenshots with timestamps, machine-fault photos, and any operator support reference. ANPR or short-stay: the operator's entry/exit images, receipts, appointment/delivery records, GPS/app history where available, and a simple timeline; distinguish driving/searching for a space from parking only if the evidence supports it.

Permit, Blue Badge or authorised visit: permit/Badge evidence, visitor records, booking or appointment proof, and relevant correspondence. Incorrect details: the full notice, vehicle records, location evidence, and a clear comparison showing the exact error. Council PCN: retain the PCN, NtO, council photos/video, relevant traffic-sign/bay photos and any document supporting the formal ground or discretionary circumstances.

For every route, retain the original notice, appeal text, attachments, submission receipt, rejection letter, verification/appeal code, and a dated case timeline.
