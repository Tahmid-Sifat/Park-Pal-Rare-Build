# Source label: evidence_request_playbook.md

Before arguing about an allegation, ask what record would prove or disprove it. For signage: ask for the sign plan, site photos, and the terms relied on. For ANPR: request the entry and exit images, timestamps and the full vehicle-registration read. For payment: retain the operator's payment record, your app/receipt/bank evidence, and any machine fault report. For a council PCN: view the authority's photographs, officer notes or available video through its official portal.

Phrase requests neutrally: "Please provide the photographs, records and terms relied on for this allegation." Identify the notice reference and the exact issue. Do not claim a record proves an error until it has been reviewed.

Build a comparison table for yourself: operator/council allegation, their evidence, your evidence, what remains uncertain, and the specific question to be answered. This keeps the appeal factual and gives judges a clear audit trail in a demo.
