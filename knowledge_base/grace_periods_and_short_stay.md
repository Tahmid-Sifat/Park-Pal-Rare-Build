# Source label: grace_periods_and_short_stay.md

A short stay or grace period argument can matter where site terms, operator codes of practice, or council policies allow a brief period to read signs, make payment, or leave. Relevance depends on the actual timed stay, camera timestamps, and whether the allegation is no-payment, overstay, or stopping where prohibited.

Helpful evidence includes ANPR entry/exit times, payment attempt screenshots, receipts with timestamps, and a clear timeline. A short stay is not automatically a winning ground; the user should match facts to the notice reason carefully.
