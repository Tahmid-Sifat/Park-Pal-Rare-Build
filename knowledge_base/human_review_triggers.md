# Source label: human_review_triggers.md

Pause the normal self-service route and seek stage-specific help when the document mentions a court claim, claim form, hearing, legal proceedings, enforcement agent, warrant, Order for Recovery, statutory declaration, vehicle removal, bankruptcy, or a deadline that may already have passed.

Also consider human support where the notice involves complex keeper/driver questions, a serious disability/accessibility issue, vulnerable circumstances, multiple linked notices, a lease/hire/fleet vehicle, or evidence that is contested or incomplete. ParkPal can organise facts and documents but should not present itself as a substitute for legal advice.

Preserve the original document and dates received. Do not ignore formal correspondence or assume a debt-collection letter, court document and original parking notice use the same response route.
