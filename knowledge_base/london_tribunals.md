# Source label: london_tribunals.md

For many London borough PCNs, after an unsuccessful challenge there may be a route to London Tribunals / an independent adjudicator. Outside London, equivalent Traffic Penalty Tribunal style routes may apply depending on the council and notice stage.

Deadlines and online portals are notice-specific. Users should keep the Notice of Rejection, PCN number, and evidence pack ready, and verify the exact filing deadline on the official letter before preparing representations.
