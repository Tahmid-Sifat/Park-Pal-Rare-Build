# Source label: payment_machine_app_issues.md

Payment-related appeals often involve a machine that was out of order, an app session that failed or was not recognised, a mistyped registration, or a tariff confusion. Operators may still reject if their records show no matching payment.

Strong evidence includes payment confirmation screenshots, bank or card statements, machine out-of-order photos, app error messages, and proof of the correct VRM attempt. State what was tried, when, and what evidence shows the attempt.
