# Source label: private_parking_overview.md

Private parking charges are not the same as council penalty charge notices. A typical route is to appeal to the parking operator first, then consider the stated escalation body if the operator rejects the appeal. The available route can depend on the operator, trade association, notice stage, and wording on the official notice.

Evidence matters. Keep the notice, photos of signs, receipts, app sessions, and any timeline showing what happened.
