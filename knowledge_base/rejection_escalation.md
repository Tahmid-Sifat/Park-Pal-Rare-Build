# Source label: rejection_escalation.md

After a parking appeal is rejected, the next step depends on the notice type and the rejection letter wording. Private operator rejections may offer an independent appeal body such as POPLA or IAS, usually with a stated deadline and sometimes a verification code. Council PCN rejections may point to formal representation or an adjudicator / tribunal route depending on the stage.

Users should extract: rejection date, stated escalation body, deadline or day-count, any code or reference, and whether evidence was addressed. Do not miss a deadline while preparing an improved evidence pack. ParkPal guidance is informational only.
